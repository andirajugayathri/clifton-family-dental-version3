From Our Family to Yours

Clifton Family Dental is a family owned and operated local dental practice conveniently located in Clifton Village Shopping Centre in Clifton Beach, Queensland.

We provide a calm, welcoming environment for all new and existing patients. Clifton Family Dental strives to ensure everyone feels comfortable in the dentist chair, and our sympathetic, experienced dentists are determined to provide excellent results in a relaxing environment, to make sure even the most anxious dental patients feel at ease.

Our caring and dedicated dentists are more than happy to take the time to talk to you about the procedures, costs and to answer any questions you may have before the treatment to make sure you feel comfortable, leading to a positive dental experience.

Because Your Convenience Matters

Flexible Payment Options

We acknowledge that certain dental treatments can be quite expensive, and can cause some patients to stress about their finances. At Clifton Family Dental, we don’t want anyone to miss out on necessary dental treatments due to financial worries, so we have flexible payment options for your peace of mind.


We are preferred providers for HCF, Medibank, NIB, and DVA. We also accept the Child Dental Benefits Schedule (CDBS). The payment plans we have available are HICAPS, National Dental Plan, EziPay and Afterpay.

Talk to one of our friendly staff members today to find out about eligibility, and which payment plan would suit your needs the best.
