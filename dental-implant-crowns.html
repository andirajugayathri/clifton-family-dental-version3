Dental Implant Crowns in Clifton Beach

Dental Implant Crowns can be a life-changing treatment for patients with severely damaged or missing teeth. Not only are they extremely durable often lasting a decade or more, but they also fully restore the function of your missing tooth so you can eat, drink and speak comfortably.

At Clifton Family Dental, we offer dental crown treatments for patients with dental implants. We also work closely with specialists who offer implant surgery and bone grafting. So if you require dental implant surgery, we can provide you with further information and a local referral.

What Is A Dental Implant Crown?

While a regular dental crown is a tooth cap used to strengthen a weakened tooth, a dental implant crown is a missing tooth replacement that is attached to an implant. Dental Implant Crowns look, feel and function just like natural teeth and can be colour-matched to blend in with the rest of your smile.

Flexible Payment Options For Dental Implant Crowns

If you are missing teeth or have a damaged tooth that needs to be removed, it is recommended that you book your missing tooth replacement consultation as soon as possible. This is to reduce the discomfort and difficulties that come with having missing teeth and prevent facial sagging that can occur over a longer period.

It is especially important that dental implant crown consultations are booked as quickly as possible. This is because getting dental implants and then dental implant crowns is a long process that requires multiple appointments over months, so it is ideal to get started as soon as you can.
