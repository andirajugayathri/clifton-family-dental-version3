Implant Restorative Treatments in Clifton Beach

Implant restorative treatments are excellent long-term solutions for missing teeth. The dental implant itself is a titanium screw inserted into the jawbone to take the place of a missing tooth's root. It is a permanent restoration that is used to secure an artificial tooth (dental restoration) in place.

Some of the most common implant-supported dental restorations that look and feel like natural teeth are dental crowns and bridges:

Dental Crowns: A tooth cap used to strengthen a weakened tooth or attach to a dental implant. They are a suitable missing tooth replacement for patients with one missing tooth.

Dental Bridges: A restoration that is used to replace multiple missing teeth in a row. Bridges are made up of a set of artificial teeth that look and feel like natural teeth and attach to dental implants, or neighbouring teeth.
