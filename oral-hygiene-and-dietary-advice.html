Hygiene Treatment in Clifton Beach

Hygiene Treatment also referred to as a Dental Scale and Clean or simply "Teeth Cleaning", is a dental treatment used to clean the teeth and remove plaque build-up. The process is quick and highly effective for removing the more stubborn stains, plaque and tartar deposits that cannot be removed with regular teeth brushing and flossing.

This simple, non-invasive treatment can significantly reduce your chances of developing dental problems such as tooth decay and gum disease. That's why we recommended patients receive Hygiene Treatment about every six months, or twice yearly as part of their regular dental check-up appointment.

Oral Hygiene and Dietary Advice

Maintaining a healthy smile requires more effort than just the occasional dental check-up. Your dental hygiene and oral care starts at home with daily tooth brushing and flossing and can be impacted by all kinds of dietary and lifestyle factors.

We are committed to providing our local community with a full range of dental care services. That's why, as well as general dentistry, our clinical team also offers oral hygiene and dietary advice to patients during consultations.

Oral Health For Overall Health

Proper oral hygiene not only prevents cavities and periodontal disease but also affects our overall health and well-being. At Clifton Family Dental we will teach you how to properly brush your teeth and show you the best techniques for maintaining good oral hygiene. We will also advise you on when to remove dental plaque which forms on your teeth in places that are harder to clean.

For families with babies or young children, our pediatric dental hygienist provides children's oral hygiene advice and resources to help you ensure that your kids' oral health is in check. We believe it is never too early to start teaching the next generation about preventative care and encouraging healthy habits.
